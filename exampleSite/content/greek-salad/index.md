## Ingredients

#### Salad

- 4 large ripe tomatoes, cut into irregular chunks
- 1 cucumber, halved lengthways and sliced
- 1 red onion, thinly sliced into half-moons
- 1 green pepper, deseeded and sliced
- 200g feta cheese, in one block
- 100g Kalamata olives
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano

#### Dressing

- 4 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
- Salt and freshly ground black pepper

## Directions

1. Cut the tomatoes into large, uneven chunks and place them in a wide shallow bowl
1. Halve the cucumber lengthways, then slice it into thick half-moons
   1. Add to the bowl with the tomatoes
1. Thinly slice the red onion into half-moons and scatter over the salad
1. Deseed and slice the green pepper, adding the slices to the bowl
1. Add the Kalamata olives
1. Place the block of feta directly on top of the salad â€” do not crumble it
   1. This is the traditional Greek way
1. Whisk together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, salt, and pepper
1. Drizzle the dressing over the salad
1. Sprinkle generously with dried oregano
1. Serve immediately with crusty bread
